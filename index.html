About

I am a French creative designer and art director currently working at London based Digital Fashion agency, Meri Media.

Over the last few years, I have gained a lot of experience and drawn my inspiration from studies, work experiences and journeys which keep enhancing my visual culture. I believe that the atmosphere around us inspires what we do, in every way - this is why I try to expand my influences as much as I can.

With an equal interest in all areas of Print, Interactive and Digital Design, and Moving Images, I always try to find the right media for every communication need. I approach every project with the aim of imbuing any idea with simplicity, precision and clarity. My goal is to create impactful experiences, linking strong design with impressive content.

If you are looking to fill any freelance work please get in touch.
